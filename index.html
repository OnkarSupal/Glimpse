Purpose

-To limit the children from watching TV
-To have analytical control over the media that children watch

Objectives

-To decrease the screen time of children of watching TV
-To provide an efficient system to track children

Scope

-To track the actual TV watch time by the children
-To monitor activities done by the children




System Architecture

-The GLIMPSE system shall be Android-based application system running in an Android environment. The system shall be developed using Android, XML and SQLite database.
-A person who has no knowledge of Android Mobiles will find it difficult to understand the system. But with a little knowledge it will be very easy to handle the project.





Conclusion

-The actual TV watch time of children will be decreased.
-The parents will have the control over their children.
- The proposed System is efficient to handle exceptions.
